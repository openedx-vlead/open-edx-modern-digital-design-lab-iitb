To Design, implement and Simulate the Flip-Flop.

Objective:-

       1. Design and test working of all flip flops.

       2. Understanding each flip flop and its advantage over the other.        

       3. Conversion from one flip flop to another.       

       4. Understand the concept and difference of a latch and a flip flop (level triggered and edge triggered circuits). Also learn best design usage of both edge and level triggered circuits.
